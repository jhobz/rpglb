General Rules:

  • Be respectful to others, whether they are attending the event or not
  • Treat the equipment and furniture with care
  • Wear your badge at all times and please make sure it is viewable.
  • Shower daily. For everybody’s sake
  • Clean up after yourself and throw away your trash
  • Keep food to the food/puzzle/board games room within the main hotel building. Food will not be allowed in the Officers Club. The room will be cleaned up an hour after catering is delivered, and leftovers are allowed to be taken up to your own room if you wish to save some for later.
  • Beverages in the kitchen are free to attendees (courtesy of NAMI). If you take the last can/bottle of a flavor out of the fridge, please put a new pack in to replace it
  • No alcohol or drunkenness in the Officer’s Club - keep that to your rooms or offsite
  • No illegal drugs. Period
  • We are not above the law, so do not break it
  • Please be aware that sleeping in the Officer’s Club is not allowed. If you’re tired then please take a nap in your hotel room. Attendees staying offsite are responsible for arranging transportation to return to where they are staying.
  • Breaking of the rules will result in a verbal warning to begin with, and moving onto more steps if repeat violations occur and depending on severity of the incidence.
  • We do not allow animals onsite except ADA qualified service animals
