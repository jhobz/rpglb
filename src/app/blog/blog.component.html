Announcing RPG Limit Break 2025!

November 27, 2024

We are happy to announce the event information for RPG Limit Break 2025! The marathon will take place May 18th - 24th in Denver, CO, and will be a hybrid event with options to participate remotely.

We have announced our Health Policy for 2025.

NAMI has been a great partner for RPGLB, providing support for the event and promoting the stream, so we're happy to announce we'll be partnering with them again for RPGLB 2025. Charity selection is something we will always re-evaluate on an annual basis, based on community & viewer feedback.

As in previous years, 100% of donations made during the event will be shared by NAMI (National Alliance on Mental Illness) at the national level and NAMI Colorado, the local NAMI chapter that works closely with our event, to provide education and support to people affected by mental illness and their families, including young adults. Both organizations raise public awareness about mental illness to reduce stigma and advocate for access to and coverage of mental health care. They provide information and resources to people around the world at https://nami.org.

RPGLB 2025 Venue

For our 2025 event, we will be moving to Denver Tech Center area in Denver, CO. A lot of time and effort went into researching not only individual hotels, but entire regions in the west half of the United States searching for our new home. We are reasonably confident that we will be in our new area for the foreseeable future. Excitingly, due to our new venue having a larger airport nearby and lower priced hotel rooms, it should also be more affordable to attend for most attendees! There's also easily accessible public transit right nearby, and plenty of locations, food and otherwise, within immediate walking distance.

The attendee cap for RPGLB 2025 will be 200 - we have an initial block of 60 hotel rooms per event night. Hotel rooms will be $119/night + tax with complimentary breakfast and parking. Room reservation details will be posted when RPGLB 2025 event registration opens on March 16, 2025.

All attendees must be at least 18 years old - minors are welcome to attend free of charge, as long as they're accompanied by a parent/guardian that is registered & paid for the event. Minors will need to comply with the Health Policy, the same as adult attendees. The amount and names of minors will need to be noted in the parent/guardian's event registration, so we can issue them a Minor badge during their attendance.

Registration Process/Attendance Costs

The attendance fee will be $75 USD for the full event. There will not be single-day passes. Registration & attendee fee payment will be done via https://rpglimitbreak.com (with Stripe used as payment processor).

Game submissions will be open February 2-18, 2025 (via https://rpglimitbreak.com) and the schedule will be posted March 16, 2025. There will not be a separate game list release or additional submission period. For more information when preparing your submission, check out our submission guidelines page.

RPGLB 2025 Remote Runs

RPGLB 2025 will be an in-person event and we will be preferring onsite runs; but in order to be inclusive to runners that are unable to attend in person, we will also allow for remote run submissions (performed from the runner's home). Remote runs will be expected to follow the same guidelines as onsite runs.
