Welcome to RPG Limit Break, a week-long marathon of RPG speedruns (finishing console-style role playing games quickly)! RPGLB is an annual event where attendees come to perform speedruns or support our efforts to raise money for {{event.cause}}. We also work closely with NAMI to help spread awareness about mental illness and the donation effort.

RPG Limit Break 2019 will run from May 5 - 11. The event takes place in Salt Lake City, UT, but anyone can watch our live-stream throughout the whole event which runs non-stop from start to finish. Be sure to check out our schedule & donation incentives, and please support the cause & spread the word!
