Health Policy for RPG Limit Break 2025

Thank you for your interest in attending RPG Limit Break 2025 (RPGLB 2025)! Our Health Policy will be a permanent fixture that replaces our COVID Policy of years prior. This policy is a living document (last updated November 27, 2024), and may be updated if public health regulations and/or safety recommendations change between now and the event.

RPG Limit Break 2025 Health and Safety Policy

  • RPGLB Staff encourages all attendees to stay up to date on COVID-19 vaccinations, per the US Center for Disease Control's guidelines. While strongly recommended, COVID-19 vaccination will not be required for attending RPGLB 2025.
  • Masks will be optional in all RPGLB 2025 event spaces. Any attendees who wish to mask while participating in RPGLB 2025 (as a runner, commentator, volunteer, etc) may do so, and RPGLB Staff will provide assistance to any accommodations said individual requires in wearing their mask in our spaces.
  • To maintain a safe & healthy environment for all RPGLB 2025 attendees, anyone who displays symptoms of contagious illness (such as heavy or frequent coughing) will be asked by the RPGLB Safety Team to wear an N-95 mask (provided by RPGLB Staff) or leave the event spaces, depending on severity of symptoms.

What else is RPG Limit Break doing to maximize attendee safety?

In addition to the above policy, we will also be doing the following:

  • We will have a dedicated team whose responsibility will be to regularly sanitize contact areas of the event space, which will be done approximately every 2 hours.
  • We will provide all attendees at check-in with masks, upon request.
  • Hand sanitizer stations will be available in all event rooms.
  • Air purifier units will be located in the Stage half of the Stream Room and in the Social Room.

If at any time you have health concerns that you observe at our event, including yourself, please reach out to safety@rpglimitbreak.com.
